Lighting Section

Design Presentation of Lighting – 2 weeks before Bump In

Operator Position – rehearsals and performance

Standard Rig – explanation of standard rig (add to it, not change it)

Bump In – (Guild – check the focus, refocus may be required)

Ceiling Access – limited access to roof cavity (front of house)

(Your design presentation should include plans or locations of position of lights, strobes, smoke machine, any other effects, lights on the floor, cables used, operator position, distances, cue synopsis, equipment list)

Risk Assessment – Strobe Lighting, Smoke Machine, any additional equipment

Follow spot – operator, communications

Hire of UHT Lighting Equipment – in addition to the Standard Rig and on Rack Equipment

Hire of External Equipment – testing and tagging

Weights of Lighting Bars – addition of equipment to existing loaded bars may not be possible

Plotting Session – cue synopsis list of each lighting cue, a script with the cues in it, and rough timing of every cue (do you have enough time) – prepare for making time of 5 minutes per lighting cue state. If you are using moving lights, there is a potential for increasing that time substantially.

Moving Lighting, Special Lighting (UV), Mirror Ball, Dry Ice

DMX (Digital Multiplex) – lighting desk control of Dimmers, Smoke Machine, Strobe, Moving Lighting, LED Lighting and their cable runs

Floor Electrician – warm up, lamp check, colour burn out replacement, fluid levels

Talkback locations – production desk

Bump Out – return to standard rig (Union or Guild)
